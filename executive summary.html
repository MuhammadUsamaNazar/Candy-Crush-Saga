Candy Crush Saga

Executive Summary:

Candy Crush Saga is a free-to-play match-three puzzle video game for Facebook; other versions for IOS, Android, Windows Phone, and Windows 10 followed. It is a variation of their browser game Candy Crush.

Project Scope:

In the game, players will complete levels by swapping colored pieces of candy on a game board to make a match of three or more of the same color, eliminating those candies from the board and replacing them with new ones, which could potentially create further matches. Matches of four or more candies will create unique candies that act as power-ups with larger board-clearing abilities. Boards will have various goals that must be completed within a fixed number of moves or limited amount of time, such as a certain score or collecting a specific number of a type of candy. And this game is a relaxing game which can help its palyer to release the stress and to enjoy different types of easy and hard levels of puzzles the player should use his/her intelligence to pass through different levels of the games.
